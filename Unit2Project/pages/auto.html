The Future of Driving?

There are self driving cars already on the road. This can be beneficial as the self driving vehicle will obey all traffic laws and could reduce the amount of accidents.

Current Limitations and Barriers

There are several limitations and barriers that could impede adoption of AVs, including lack of buyer demand, data security, protection against cyberattacks, regulations compatible with driverless operation, resolved liability laws, societal attitude and behavior change regarding distrust and subsequent resistance to AV use, and the development of economically viable AV technologies.6 Weather can adversely affect sensor performance on AVs, potentially impeding adoption. Ford recognized this barrier and started conducting AV testing in the snow in 2016 at Mcity, utilizing technologies suited for poor weather.
